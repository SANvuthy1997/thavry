Everything in one bag

When I travel from 8 days to 12 days, then my backpack is only about 3 to 4.5 kg of weight. I make sure to pack all the things I will need during my trip. When it comes to packing for traveling, I would spend about 20 minutes to put all things in the bag. I don’t need to bother choosing what type of cloth, or dress to bring.
